Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

Welcome to UniRate.

UniRate is a currency and cryptocurrency exchange rate application that allows users to view and convert exchange rates using publicly available financial data.

Information Collection and Use

UniRate does not require account registration or user login.

We do not collect personally identifiable information such as:

  • Full names
  • Email addresses
  • Phone numbers
  • Payment information
  • Government identification data

The application is designed to function without requiring personal user data.

Exchange Rate Data

UniRate retrieves currency and cryptocurrency exchange rate information from third-party APIs and data providers.

Advertisements

The application may display third-party advertisements in certain versions of the app.

Third-party advertising providers may collect limited device-related information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Future Premium Features

UniRate may introduce optional premium or subscription-based features in future updates.

Any payment processing for subscriptions will be securely handled by Google Play and not directly by UniRate.

Data Security

We aim to maintain a secure and reliable application experience.

Children's Privacy

UniRate is not directed toward children under the age of 13.
